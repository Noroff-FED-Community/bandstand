About Polaris

Polaris is a metalcore band hailing from Sydney, Australia. Formed in 2012, the band quickly gained recognition for their energetic live performances and powerful songwriting.

With their signature blend of heavy breakdowns, melodic hooks, and introspective lyrics, Polaris has become one of the leading bands in the modern metalcore scene.

Discography

  • Album: "The Mortal Coil" (2017)
  • Album: "The Death of Me" (2020)
